ABOUT BELINSOFT
BELINSOFT is a company of software and hardware solutions and services related to technical and cryptographic data protection, covert information interception and social engineering, public and industrial security.
The main ones are strategic and tactical data interception tools, cybersecurity solutions and special systems that secure state borders and critical infrastructures.
BELINSOFT's advantageous approach ensures the delivery of specialized turnkey solutions that meet the highest customer requirements.
Cooperation with BELINSOFT provides a unique advantage - the development and turnkey delivery of customized integrated solutions. The company's products accumulate the latest global high-tech developments related to cybersecurity, other related specific activities and targeted R&D.

LAWFUL INTERCEPTION & DPI SYSTEMS
End-to-end solutions for the entire intelligence cycle, including collection, processing, analysis and visualization of information. The Systems provide with full spectrum intelligence capabilities and can be used nationwide for national security protection.
WEBINT SYSTEMS
Comprehensive Web intelligence solutions interact with all the web to provide the most advanced intelligence. Our WEBINT Systems analyze and visualize vast amounts of information from multiple sources and empower users with ability to predict and investigate existing and developing threats.
GEOLOCATION SYSTEM
Geolocation System allow to determine in real-time the location of any subscriber of almost any mobile network in the world regardless the home network or roaming. The System supports GSM, UMTS and LTE networks and provides fast operating and accurate results.
WI-FI INTELLIGENCE SYSTEM
Wi-Fi Intelligence System is designed to enable field agents to access and collect target data via Wi-Fi networks. The System relies on advanced communication and cyber intelligence solutions to overcome various operational challenges.
SATCOM INTELLIGENCE SYSTEM
Satellite Communication Intelligence System is a highly sophisticated automated solution designed for intercepting communicationin popular satellite networks and for conducting near real-time analysis of satellite communication channels of interest.
SECURITY OPERATIONS CENTER
The Center for Monitoring and Prevention of Information Security Incidents is a combination of technologies, processes and human resources aimed at preventing, timely identifying, responding to and minimizing the consequences of computer attacks on critical important informatization objects.
TRAINING AND SIMULATION PLATFORM
Training and simulation platform is a hardware and software solution designed for training and skills improvement of information security specialists. The platform uses a virtual environment and allows to emulate various large-scale multi-user information systems as well as play different cyberattack scenarios in close to real life conditions.
SECURE COMMUNICATION SYSTEMS
Secure communication systems include a full range of scalable and flexible solutions to ensure security and anonymity and avoid data leaks.  There are solutions for secure working environment for high sensitive projects, secure internet browsing, secure messaging, etc.
BORDER SECURITY SYSTEM
Border security system is a complete solution for protection of state border from external and internal intruders. The system includes a variety of technical subsystems and Command & Control center with advanced C4I capabilities.
SAFE CITY SYSTEM
Safe City is an information and analytical system that consists of many subsystems operating in automatic mode and providing control, processing and coordination of a vast number of processes related to public security and life in urban environment.
ANTI-UAV SYSTEM
ANTI-UAV System is designed to counteract against rotor and wing UAVs for protecting related key area and responding to the threats like terrorist attacks, drug trafficking and aerial photography.The System identifies illegal UAVs, jams their communication channels and makes them stop the flight and land.
